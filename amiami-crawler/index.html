                  About

                  Progress indicator is displayed in the bottom left corner. Items display as they come in.
                  Icons link to the preowned product's page, the name link leads to the general page of the product - these later will be revised to open a modal with an iframe to the product page so you can waste money without changing tabs.
                  The tiny red × at the bottom of cards is a blacklist button, which will blacklist item codes like GOODS-01234 from appearing on lists other than blacklisted and past diff lists.
                  The input field at the top left corner is a fuzzy search box, meaning that it will match exact or similar products based on their name.
